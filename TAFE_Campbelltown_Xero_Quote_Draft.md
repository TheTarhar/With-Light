## Assumptions and Qualifications
- Quote is based on tender documentation reviewed only, not a full measured take-off.
- Pricing assumes normal working hours and unobstructed site access.
- Existing concealed services condition is assumed to be generally consistent with the documents.
- No asbestos removal, hazardous material remediation, or latent condition allowance included unless specifically noted in the tender documents.
- Major authority upgrades, utility relocations, and structural remedial works outside the issued scope are excluded unless clearly shown.
- FF&E supply is excluded unless specifically required for our installed equipment.
- This is a trade/subcontract quote only and not the builder's full project value.
