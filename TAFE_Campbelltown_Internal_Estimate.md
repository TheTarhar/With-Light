## Pricing Logic
This estimate has been built from the tender scope signals rather than using only a project percentage rule. The documents indicate a moderate-to-heavy electrical package including new distribution boards, workshop power infrastructure, lighting changes, security recabling, comms cabinet works, camera integration, and multiple AV spaces. The figure includes direct install costs plus procurement, supervision, travel, documentation, and a controlled contingency for refurbishment unknowns.

## Commercial Position
- Aggressive floor: $289,000 + GST
- Balanced submission: $313,000 + GST
- Safer position: $328,000 + GST

## Key Assumptions
- Normal working hours and reasonable site access
- Existing services generally align with tender documentation
- No asbestos or hazardous materials removal in our scope
- No major authority augmentation or structural remedial works in our scope
- Security equipment supply by others where noted in the tender documents remains unchanged

## Risk Notes
Main pricing risks are concealed existing services, access restrictions in a live education environment, design development after tender, and coordination complexity around workshop power, security migration, and AV integration.
